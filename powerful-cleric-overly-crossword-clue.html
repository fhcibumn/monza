The answer to the "powerful cleric overly" crossword clue is ARCHBISHOP.

Who is an Archbishop?

An archbishop is a high-ranking bishop who is in charge of an archdiocese. In the Catholic Church, an archbishop is responsible for overseeing the work of other bishops in their province or region. They are also responsible for administering the sacraments and ensuring the spiritual well-being of their flock.

How is an Archbishop Appointed?

Archbishops are appointed by the Pope, who selects them based on their experience, education, and the needs of the Church. Once appointed, an archbishop is ordained by a bishop and assumes his new role.

What is the Role of an Archbishop?

As we mentioned earlier, the role of an archbishop is to oversee the work of other bishops in their region. They also play an important role in promoting unity and cooperation among different dioceses, and may be called upon to resolve disputes or provide guidance on matters of doctrine.

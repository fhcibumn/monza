Cinco De Mayo Kid Activities - News Blog

Cinco De Mayo is a popular Mexican holiday celebrated on the 5th of May every year. This holiday commemorates the Mexican army's victory over the French at the Battle of Puebla in 1862. It is a day of celebration with parades, music, food, and fun activities for everyone, especially kids. If you're looking for some great Cinco De Mayo kid activities, then you're in the right place! In this article, we will explore some of the best activities you can do with your kids to celebrate this holiday.

Make Your Own Piñata

Piñatas are a must-have item for any Cinco De Mayo celebration. Kids love them! Instead of buying a piñata, why not make your own with your kids? You can use papier-mâché, balloons, and tissue paper to create a colorful and unique piñata. Fill it with treats and let the kids take turns hitting it until it breaks and spills all the goodies. It's a fun and creative way to get everyone involved in the festivities.

Cinco De Mayo Crafts

Kids love to get creative, and Cinco De Mayo is the perfect occasion to do so. You can make Mexican flags, paper flowers, maracas, or sombreros with your kids. There are plenty of online tutorials and templates available that you can use. Crafting is a great way to bond with your kids and teach them about different cultures and traditions.

Cinco De Mayo Cooking

Cooking is another fun activity to do with your kids on Cinco De Mayo. You can make traditional Mexican dishes such as tacos, guacamole, salsa, and quesadillas. Let the kids help with the cooking and allow them to create their own toppings and fillings. It's a great way to introduce them to new flavors and cuisines.

Cinco De Mayo Games

Games are always a hit with kids, and there are plenty of Cinco De Mayo themed games you can play. You can set up a piñata game, a beanbag toss, or a Mexican hat dance competition. You can also organize a scavenger hunt and hide Mexican themed items around the house or yard. It's a great way to keep the kids entertained and active.

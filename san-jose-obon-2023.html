What is San Jose Obon?

San Jose Obon is an annual festival that celebrates Japanese culture and tradition. The festival is held in San Jose, California and is a time for the Japanese community to come together and honor their ancestors.

Obon is a Buddhist festival that originated in Japan and is usually held in August. The festival is a time for families to come together, visit their ancestors' graves, and participate in traditional dances called Bon Odori.

When is San Jose Obon 2023?

San Jose Obon 2023 will be held on August 5th and 6th at the San Jose Buddhist Church Betsuin. The festival typically runs from noon to 10 pm on both days and is free to attend.

Visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, including traditional Japanese food, arts and crafts, and cultural exhibits. There will also be live music and performances throughout the event.

What to Expect at San Jose Obon 2023?

San Jose Obon 2023 promises to be a fun-filled event for all ages. Visitors can expect to see a variety of cultural performances, including taiko drumming, martial arts demonstrations, and traditional Japanese dance.

If you want to get involved in San Jose Obon 2023, there are a few ways to do so. You can volunteer to help with the event, participate in the Bon Odori dance, or even become a vendor and sell your own Japanese-inspired creations.

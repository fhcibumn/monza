Homemade Fruit Tree Spray | News Blog - 2023

Are you tired of spending a fortune on fruit tree sprays? Look no further than your own kitchen for a solution. Homemade fruit tree sprays are not only cost-effective, but also free from harmful chemicals.

Here are four different homemade fruit tree sprays you can make at home:

Garlic and Pepper Spray

This spray is effective in repelling insects and pests. To make the spray, blend 10 cloves of garlic, 1 tablespoon of cayenne pepper, and 1 quart of water. Strain the mixture and pour it into a spray bottle. Spray the mixture onto your fruit trees and repeat every two weeks.

Oil Spray

This spray can help control pests such as mites, aphids, and scales. Mix 1 cup of vegetable oil and 1 tablespoon of mild soap in a gallon of water. Shake well before using and spray the mixture onto the leaves and branches of your fruit trees. Repeat every two weeks.

Milk Spray

This spray is effective in controlling powdery mildew. Mix 1 part milk and 2 parts water in a spray bottle. Spray the mixture onto the leaves of your fruit trees every two weeks.

Vinegar Spray

This spray can help control fungal diseases such as black spot and apple scab. Mix 3 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar with 1 gallon of water. Spray the mixture onto the leaves and branches of your fruit trees every two weeks.

These homemade fruit tree sprays are not only easy to make, but also safe for your fruit trees and the environment. Give them a try and enjoy a bountiful harvest.

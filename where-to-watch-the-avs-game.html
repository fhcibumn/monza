Where to Watch the AVS Game - 2023 News Blog Article

The Colorado Avalanche (AVS) are one of the most exciting teams in the NHL and fans are always looking for ways to watch their games. Whether you're a die-hard fan or just looking to catch a game, here are some of the best places to watch the AVS play in 2023.

With the rise of streaming services, watching the AVS game has never been easier. One of the best options is to subscribe to NHL.tv. This service allows you to watch every game live or on-demand, so you never have to miss a moment. Plus, you can watch on your TV, computer, or mobile device, making it easy to catch a game anywhere.

If you're looking for a more social experience, heading to a sports bar is a great option. In Denver, there are plenty of great bars to choose from, including Blake Street Tavern and The Sportsbook Bar & Grill. These bars offer a lively atmosphere, great food and drinks, and plenty of TVs to watch the game.

Catching the Game at the Pepsi Center

If you're lucky enough to be in Denver, catching the game at the Pepsi Center is an unforgettable experience. The home of the AVS, the Pepsi Center offers a high-energy atmosphere and a chance to see your favorite players up close. Plus, there are plenty of food and drink options available, so you can make a night of it.

Watching the AVS on Local TV

If you don't have access to streaming services or can't make it to a sports bar, watching the AVS on local TV is still an option. Altitude TV is the official broadcaster of the AVS and broadcasts the majority of their games. Check your local listings to see when and where you can catch the game on TV.

Streaming the Game on Social Media

Finally, another option to watch the AVS game is by streaming it on social media. Many fans will live stream the game on platforms like Facebook or Instagram, allowing you to watch the game for free. While the quality may not be as good as other options, it's still a great way to catch the game if you're on a budget.

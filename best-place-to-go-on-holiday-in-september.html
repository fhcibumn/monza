Best Places to Go on Holiday in September - 2023

September is a wonderful month to go on holiday. The summer crowds have disappeared, the weather is still warm, and the prices are more affordable. But with so many options available, where should you go? In this article, we have compiled some of the best places to go on holiday in September.

Italy - Amalfi Coast

September is the perfect time to visit the Amalfi Coast in Italy. The weather is still warm, but the crowds have dissipated, making it a more enjoyable experience. You can spend your days exploring the beautiful towns of Positano, Amalfi, and Ravello, while enjoying the delicious Italian cuisine. Don't forget to take a boat tour along the coast for a truly breathtaking experience.

The Amalfi Coast is also known for its stunning beaches, so don't forget to pack your swimsuit. And if you're a fan of hiking, the Path of the Gods is a must-do activity, offering stunning views of the coastline.

Overall, the Amalfi Coast is a perfect destination for a relaxing holiday in September.

Greece - Santorini

Santorini is one of the most beautiful islands in Greece and September is the perfect time to visit. The crowds have dispersed, the weather is still warm, and the sea is calm. You can spend your days exploring the charming villages of Oia and Fira, while admiring the famous white-washed buildings and the stunning views of the Aegean Sea.

Make sure to visit the Red Beach and the Black Beach for a unique beach experience. And if you're a wine lover, don't miss the chance to visit some of the local wineries and taste the famous Santorini wines.

Santorini is a perfect destination for a romantic holiday or a relaxing getaway with friends.

Spain - Barcelona

Barcelona is a vibrant city with a perfect blend of culture, history, and modernity. September is a great time to visit the city, as the weather is still warm, but not too hot, and the crowds have dispersed. You can spend your days exploring the famous landmarks such as the Sagrada Familia and Park Guell, while also indulging in the delicious Spanish cuisine.

If you're a football fan, you can't miss the chance to visit the Camp Nou Stadium, home of the famous Barcelona FC. And if you're looking for some beach time, Barceloneta Beach is just a short walk from the city center.

Overall, Barcelona is a perfect destination for a city break in September.

Thailand - Phuket

Phuket is one of the most popular destinations in Thailand, and September is a great time to visit. The weather is still warm and the sea is calm, making it a perfect time for water activities such as snorkeling and scuba diving. You can also explore the beautiful beaches and the local markets, while indulging in the delicious Thai cuisine.

If you're looking for some adventure, you can take a trip to the nearby islands of Phi Phi and James Bond Island. And for a more relaxing experience, you can visit some of the local spas and enjoy a traditional Thai massage.

Phuket is a perfect destination for a beach holiday in September.

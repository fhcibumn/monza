How To Apply For Apec Card In Singapore: A Comprehensive Guide For 2023

Are you a frequent traveler to Asia-Pacific countries for business purposes? If yes, then you must have heard about the APEC card. The APEC Business Travel Card (ABTC) is an excellent initiative by the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) that allows business travelers to enjoy visa-free entry into participating countries. In this article, we will guide you on how to apply for an APEC card in Singapore, one of the participating countries.

What is the APEC Card?

The APEC card is a travel document that allows business travelers to enter participating countries without the need for a visa. The card is valid for three years and can be used for multiple entries. The cardholders can also enjoy expedited immigration clearance and access to designated lanes at major airports.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for an APEC card, you must be a citizen of a participating APEC economy and engaged in business activities. You must also have a valid passport with at least six months’ validity and not have any criminal convictions. For Singapore citizens, you must be either a registered business owner or an employee of a registered business in Singapore.

Application Process

The application process for an APEC card in Singapore is straightforward. You can apply online through the Singapore Customs website. You will need to provide your personal details, passport information, and employment details. You will also need to upload a passport-sized photograph and a scanned copy of your passport. Once you have submitted your application, you will receive an email confirmation along with a reference number. You can use this reference number to track the status of your application online. The processing time for an APEC card is usually around two weeks.

Renewal of APEC Card

The APEC card is valid for three years from the date of issue. You can apply for a renewal of your card six months before the expiry date. The renewal process is similar to the application process, and you will need to provide updated personal and employment details.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the APEC card is an excellent initiative for business travelers who frequently visit participating APEC economies. The application process for an APEC card in Singapore is straightforward and can be done online. We hope this guide has provided you with all the necessary information on how to apply for an APEC card in Singapore.

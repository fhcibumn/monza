Shere Khan is one of the most iconic characters from The Jungle Book. He is known for his fierce personality, cunning tactics, and striking appearance. But have you ever wondered what kind of animal Shere Khan is?

Well, in the original book by Rudyard Kipling, Shere Khan is described as a Bengal tiger. This explains his orange coat with black stripes and his sharp claws and teeth. Tigers are known for their strength and agility, which is why Shere Khan is such a formidable foe for Mowgli and the other animals in the jungle.

However, in the 1967 Disney animated movie, Shere Khan is portrayed as a Bengal tiger with a slight variation in his appearance.

Clean and Maintain your Contact Lenses with Salt Water Solution

Are you one of those people who wear contact lenses on a daily basis? If yes, then you must know how important it is to take care of your lenses. Proper cleaning and maintenance of the lenses can not only prolong their life but also protect your eyes from infections and other complications. And when it comes to cleaning, nothing can beat the good old salt water solution.

Salt water solution, also known as saline solution, is a natural disinfectant and can effectively remove dirt, bacteria, and other impurities from the lenses. It is also gentle on the eyes and does not cause any irritation or discomfort. Moreover, it is easy to make at home and is much cheaper than the commercial lens cleaners available in the market.

To make the salt water solution, all you need is distilled water and non-iodized salt. Mix one teaspoon of salt in a cup of distilled water and stir until the salt dissolves completely. Your homemade saline solution is ready to use. You can store it in a clean container and use it to clean your lenses every day.

Benefits of Using Salt Water Solution

Using salt water solution to clean your contact lenses has several benefits. Firstly, it is a natural disinfectant and does not contain any harmful chemicals that can damage your lenses or eyes. Secondly, it is cost-effective and can save you a lot of money in the long run. Thirdly, it is easy to make at home and does not require any special equipment or skills.

How to Store Your Contact Lenses Properly?

Storing your contact lenses properly is as important as cleaning them. Improper storage can lead to contamination and infections. Here are some tips to store your lenses safely:

  • Always use fresh solution to store your lenses. Do not reuse the old solution.
  • Make sure the storage case is clean and dry before keeping the lenses in it.
  • Do not mix different types of solutions or use expired solutions.
  • Replace the storage case every three months to avoid bacterial growth.
  • Store your lenses in a cool and dry place away from direct sunlight.

Conclusion

Proper cleaning and maintenance of your contact lenses are crucial for your eye health. Using salt water solution is a simple and effective way to clean your lenses without causing any harm to your eyes. Moreover, proper storage of the lenses is equally important to prevent infections and other complications. Follow the above tips and enjoy clear and healthy vision with your contact lenses.

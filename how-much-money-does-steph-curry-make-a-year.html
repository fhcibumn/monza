How Much Money Does Steph Curry Make a Year?

Steph Curry is one of the most popular and successful basketball players of his time. He has won numerous awards, including two NBA MVP awards and three NBA championships with the Golden State Warriors. But with such success comes a hefty paycheck. So, just how much money does Steph Curry make a year?

As of 2023, Steph Curry's annual salary is $43.6 million. This makes him one of the highest-paid players in the NBA. However, his salary is not the only source of income for the basketball superstar.

Endorsements and Investments

In addition to his salary, Steph Curry earns a significant amount of money from endorsements and investments. He has deals with companies such as Under Armour, Chase, and JBL, to name a few. His endorsement deals alone are estimated to earn him around $45 million per year. Curry also has investments in companies like Pinterest, which have helped him increase his net worth.

Business Ventures

Steph Curry has also ventured into business, co-founding a production company called Unanimous Media. The company produces films, television shows, and documentaries. Curry also has a stake in several other businesses, including a startup that aims to revolutionize the way athletes recover from injuries.

Charitable Work

Steph Curry is known for his philanthropic efforts. He and his wife, Ayesha, have their own charity called the Eat. Learn. Play. Foundation. The foundation's mission is to provide young people with the resources and opportunities they need to thrive. Curry also supports other charitable organizations and has donated millions of dollars to causes such as education and disaster relief.

The Bottom Line

Steph Curry's annual income is certainly impressive, but it's important to remember that it's a result of his hard work, dedication, and success. From his salary as an NBA player to his endorsement deals, business ventures, and charitable work, Curry has built a brand and a legacy that extend far beyond the basketball court.

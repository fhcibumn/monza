Cast of Jane Got a Gun

In 2023, the cast of Jane Got a Gun is still being talked about. The movie, which was released a few years ago, starred some of the biggest names in Hollywood. Let's take a closer look at the cast and their performances in the film.

Natalie Portman: Jane Hammond

Natalie Portman plays the lead role of Jane Hammond, a tough and resilient woman who seeks the help of her ex-lover to save her family from a gang of outlaws. Portman's performance in the movie was praised by critics and audiences alike. She brought a sense of vulnerability and strength to the character, making her one of the most memorable heroines in recent years.

Joel Edgerton: Dan Frost

Joel Edgerton plays the role of Dan Frost, Jane's ex-lover who reluctantly agrees to help her. Edgerton's portrayal of Dan was nuanced and complex, showing a man torn between his love for Jane and his loyalty to his own gang. His performance was one of the highlights of the movie, earning him critical acclaim and cementing his status as one of the most talented actors of his generation.

Ewan McGregor: John Bishop

Ewan McGregor plays the role of John Bishop, the leader of the gang that is after Jane's family. McGregor's performance as the villainous Bishop was chilling and intense, making him one of the most memorable bad guys in recent movie history. His scenes with Portman and Edgerton were particularly gripping, showcasing his range as an actor.

Rodrigo Santoro: Fitchum

Rodrigo Santoro plays the role of Fitchum, one of Bishop's henchmen. Santoro's performance in the movie was small but powerful, showing a man who is torn between his loyalty to Bishop and his own conscience. His scenes with McGregor were some of the most tense and memorable in the movie.

The Ingredients for Mousse au Chocolat Cyril Lignac Tous en Cuisine

Before you get started on making the Mousse au Chocolat Cyril Lignac Tous en Cuisine, you need to gather all the ingredients. You will need 200 grams of dark chocolate, 6 eggs, 80 grams of sugar, and 50 grams of butter. Make sure you have all these ingredients before you start.

The first step is to melt the chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl over a pan of simmering water. Once melted, set it aside to cool. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until pale and fluffy. In another bowl, whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form.

Next, fold the melted chocolate into the egg yolk mixture, and then gently fold in the egg whites. Pour the mixture into individual serving glasses, and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours to set. Once set, serve with whipped cream and fresh berries.

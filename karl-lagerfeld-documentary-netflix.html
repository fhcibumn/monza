Karl Lagerfeld Documentary On Netflix: A Must-Watch For Fashion Enthusiasts

Netflix has done it again with their latest release, a documentary about the life and legacy of the late fashion icon Karl Lagerfeld. Lagerfeld was known for his extravagant designs, his trademark sunglasses, and his controversial opinions. He passed away in 2019, leaving behind a legacy that has influenced the fashion industry for decades. The documentary, simply titled "Karl Lagerfeld," takes a deep dive into the life of this fashion legend and is a must-watch for anyone interested in fashion or design.

Early Life and Career

The documentary begins with a look at Lagerfeld's early life in Germany and his move to Paris in the 1950s. It explores his early career as a freelance designer and his collaborations with some of the biggest names in fashion, including Fendi and Chanel. It also delves into his personal life, including his relationships and his love of books and art.

One of the most interesting parts of the documentary is the way it shows how Lagerfeld's designs evolved over time. It takes a close look at his early work and shows how he developed his signature style over the years. It also explores his use of new materials and techniques, and how he was always pushing the boundaries of what was possible in fashion.

The Chanel Years

Of course, no discussion of Karl Lagerfeld would be complete without a deep dive into his time at Chanel. The documentary spends a significant amount of time exploring Lagerfeld's 36 years at the iconic fashion house, from his first collection in 1983 to his final show in 2019. It shows how Lagerfeld transformed the brand, bringing it into the modern era while still staying true to its roots.

The documentary also includes interviews with some of Lagerfeld's closest collaborators at Chanel, including Anna Wintour and Carine Roitfeld. They offer insights into what it was like to work with Lagerfeld and how he always pushed them to be their best.

Legacy and Influence

Finally, the documentary explores Lagerfeld's legacy and his ongoing influence on the fashion industry. It shows how Lagerfeld's designs continue to inspire designers today, and how his vision for fashion has shaped the way we think about style and beauty.

Overall, the "Karl Lagerfeld" documentary is a fascinating look into the life of one of the greatest designers of our time. It's a must-watch for anyone interested in fashion or design, and a fitting tribute to a true icon.

Conclusion

Karl Lagerfeld's influence on the fashion industry is undeniable, and this documentary does an excellent job of capturing his legacy. Whether you're a die-hard fashion fan or simply interested in learning more about one of the most influential designers of the past century, this documentary is a must-watch. So fire up your Netflix account and get ready to be inspired by the life and work of Karl Lagerfeld.

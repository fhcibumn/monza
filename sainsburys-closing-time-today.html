Sainsburys Closing Time Today - News Blog 2023

Closing Time Schedule

As of January 2023, Sainsburys has made some changes to their store closing times. Majority of their stores are now closing at 10pm on weekdays and Saturdays, and at 4pm on Sundays. However, some stores may have different closing times, so it’s important to check with your local store before heading out.

These changes in closing times were made to provide a better work-life balance for their employees, whilst still accommodating the needs of their customers.

Online Shopping

For those who prefer to shop online, Sainsburys offers a convenient delivery service where you can order groceries and have them delivered straight to your doorstep. You can choose a delivery slot that suits your schedule, making it easy to work around your busy day. Delivery slots are available from 7am to 11pm, seven days a week.

Click and Collect

If you don’t have time for a full grocery shop, Sainsburys also offers a Click and Collect service. Simply order your groceries online and collect them from your local store at a time that suits you. This service is available from 8am to 10pm, seven days a week.

Bank Holiday Opening Hours

It’s worth noting that Sainsburys may have different opening and closing times during bank holidays. To avoid disappointment, make sure to check with your local store for their bank holiday schedule.

Conclusion

Knowing the Sainsburys closing time schedule can help you plan your day and avoid any last-minute rushes to the store. Whether you prefer to shop in-store or online, Sainsburys offers a range of options to suit your needs.

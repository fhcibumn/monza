When it comes to making decisions or stating opinions, it is crucial to have exact authority. Exact authority refers to having precise knowledge or expertise on a particular subject matter. In today's rapidly changing world, where information is readily available, it is more important than ever to ensure that we have exact authority before making any decisions or statements.

Exact authority can come from various sources such as education, experience, or expertise. For instance, a doctor has exact authority when it comes to medical advice, while a lawyer has exact authority when it comes to legal matters. Having exact authority not only helps in making informed decisions but also builds credibility and trust in the eyes of others.

It is essential to note that having exact authority does not mean that one knows everything about a particular subject matter. It simply means that one has the necessary knowledge or expertise to make informed decisions or statements. It is also important to be transparent about one's exact authority and to acknowledge when there are gaps in knowledge.

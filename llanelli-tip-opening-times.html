New Year, New Tip Opening Times

The Llanelli tip, also known as the Trostre Recycling Centre, is operated by Carmarthenshire County Council. As of January 2023, the tip will be open from 8:30am to 5:00pm on weekdays, and from 8:30am to 4:00pm on weekends. This is a slight change from the previous opening times, which saw the tip close earlier on weekdays.

It's worth noting that the Llanelli tip is closed on Christmas Day, Boxing Day, and New Year's Day, so plan your waste disposal accordingly if these holidays fall on a weekend.

What Can You Dispose of at the Llanelli Tip?

The Llanelli tip accepts a wide range of household waste, including garden waste, wood, metal, and electrical items. You can also dispose of bulky items, such as furniture and mattresses, at the tip. However, it's important to note that some items are not accepted, such as asbestos, gas cylinders, and commercial waste. If you're unsure whether your waste can be disposed of at the Llanelli tip, check the council's website for a full list of accepted items.

How to Use the Llanelli Tip Safely

When visiting the Llanelli tip, it's important to follow the council's safety guidelines to avoid accidents or injuries. This includes wearing appropriate clothing and footwear, keeping children and pets in the vehicle, and following the instructions of the on-site staff. It's also worth noting that the tip can get busy at peak times, so be prepared to queue and allow plenty of time for your visit.

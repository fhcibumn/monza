If you're in the market for a new van, you might be wondering about the dimensions of the Peugeot Expert. After all, when it comes to commercial vehicles, size matters. Whether you're transporting goods or people, it's important to know exactly how much space you have to work with. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the dimensions intérieures of the Peugeot Expert and what they mean for you.

The Overall Dimensions

Let's start with the basics: the overall dimensions of the Peugeot Expert. This van comes in three lengths: compact, standard, and long. The compact version measures 4.6 meters long, while the standard and long versions are 4.95 meters and 5.3 meters long, respectively. In terms of height, the Peugeot Expert stands at 1.9 meters tall. As for width, it's 2.2 meters with mirrors and 1.9 meters without.

The Load Area

Of course, the most important dimensions when it comes to a commercial van are those of the load area. The Peugeot Expert has a load length of 2.16 meters in the compact version, 2.51 meters in the standard version, and 2.86 meters in the long version. The load width is 1.6 meters, while the load height is 1.4 meters. With these dimensions, the Peugeot Expert can carry a payload of up to 1400 kg.

The Passenger Area

If you're using the Peugeot Expert to transport people rather than goods, you'll be interested in the dimensions of the passenger area. The van can accommodate up to nine people, including the driver. In the standard and long versions, there are three rows of seats, while the compact version has two. The front row is spacious, with plenty of head and legroom, while the back rows are more suited to children or smaller adults.

The Engine Options

Another important factor to consider when choosing a van is the engine. The Peugeot Expert is available with a range of diesel engines, including a 1.6L BlueHDi 95, a 1.6L BlueHDi 115, and a 2.0L BlueHDi 120. The most powerful option is the 2.0L BlueHDi 180, which is only available with the long version of the van. This engine provides plenty of power for heavy loads and long journeys.

The Technology

Finally, let's take a look at the technology that comes with the Peugeot Expert. This van is equipped with a range of features designed to make your life easier, including a touchscreen infotainment system, a rearview camera, and parking sensors. There's also the option to add advanced safety features such as lane departure warning and automatic emergency braking.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Peugeot Expert is a versatile and practical van that's well-suited to a range of commercial uses. With its range of engine options, spacious passenger area, and generous load area, it's a great choice for anyone in need of a reliable and capable vehicle. By taking the time to understand the dimensions intérieures of the Peugeot Expert, you can make an informed decision and choose the right van for your needs.

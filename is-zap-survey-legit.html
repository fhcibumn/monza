Online surveys have become a popular way for people to earn extra cash or rewards. However, with so many survey sites out there, it can be hard to determine which ones are legitimate. One site that has gained attention in recent years is Zap Survey. In this article, we will review whether Zap Survey is legit or not, based on our research and personal experience.

What is Zap Survey?

Zap Survey is a survey site that rewards users for completing surveys, participating in focus groups, and other activities. The site claims to pay out cash rewards and gift cards from popular retailers like Amazon, Walmart, and Target. Users can also donate their earnings to charity if they choose to.

Topic 1: Payment and Rewards

One of the most important things to consider when determining if a survey site is legit is its payment and rewards system. Zap Survey offers cash rewards and gift cards, which is a good sign. However, some users have reported that it takes a long time to receive their rewards or that they never received them at all. It's important to note that Zap Survey does have a minimum payout threshold of $25, which may take some time to reach depending on how many surveys you qualify for.

Topic 2: Survey Availability

Another factor to consider is the availability of surveys. Zap Survey claims to have a large pool of surveys available, but some users have reported that they don't qualify for many of them. This can be frustrating for users who are looking to earn rewards quickly. However, this is a common issue with survey sites in general and not necessarily a sign that Zap Survey is a scam.

Topic 3: User Experience

The user experience is also an important factor to consider when determining if a survey site is legit. Zap Survey has a user-friendly website and app that makes it easy to navigate and find available surveys. The site also has a referral program that rewards users for inviting friends to join. However, some users have reported technical issues with the app and website, which can be frustrating.

Topic 4: Customer Support

Finally, it's important to consider the level of customer support provided by the survey site. Zap Survey offers email support and has a FAQ section on its website. However, some users have reported slow response times or unhelpful customer service. This is something to keep in mind if you run into any issues while using the site.

In conclusion, based on our research and personal experience, Zap Survey appears to be a legit survey site. While there may be some issues with payment and survey availability, these are common issues with survey sites in general. Overall, Zap Survey offers a user-friendly platform and rewards users for completing surveys and other activities.

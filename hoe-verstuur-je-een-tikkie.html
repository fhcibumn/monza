Are you struggling to pay your friends back for that amazing weekend getaway? Or maybe you're trying to split the bill for a night out with your squad? In today's cashless society, sending money has never been easier with the help of the Dutch payment app, Tikkie. But if you're new to the app, it can be overwhelming to figure out how to use it. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of sending a Tikkie payment, so you can easily pay your friends back and enjoy life stress-free.

Step One: Download the Tikkie App

The first step to sending a Tikkie payment is to download the app. You can find it on the App Store or Google Play Store. Once you've downloaded the app, create an account by entering your personal details and linking your bank account. This process is quick and easy, and you only have to do it once.

Step Two: Create a Tikkie Request

To create a Tikkie request, open the app and click on the "Create Tikkie" button. Enter the amount you want your friend to pay you back, add a description or message if you'd like, and select the friend you want to send the request to. You can also choose to split the payment with multiple people if necessary. Once you've finished filling out the details, click "Send" and your friend will receive the Tikkie request.

Step Three: Receive Payment

Once your friend has received the Tikkie request, they can easily pay you back by clicking on the link in the message. They'll be directed to their own Tikkie app, where they can select their bank and complete the payment. You'll receive a notification once the payment has been completed, and the money will be transferred directly to your bank account. It's that simple!

Other Tikkie Features

Splitting Bills

Not only can you request money from your friends, but you can also split bills with them using Tikkie. Simply create a Tikkie request for the total amount of the bill, and select the friends you want to split it with. They'll receive a message with a link to pay their portion of the bill, and you'll receive the full amount in your bank account once everyone has paid.

Donations

Tikkie also makes it easy to donate to your favorite charities or causes. Simply select the charity you want to donate to, enter the amount you want to donate, and click "Send". You'll be directed to your bank's payment environment, where you can complete the donation securely and easily. Tikkie even sends you a receipt for your donation for tax purposes.

Online Shopping

Finally, Tikkie makes online shopping easy and secure. Many online retailers now accept Tikkie as a payment method, so you can complete your purchase quickly and easily without having to enter your credit card information. Simply select Tikkie as your payment method at checkout, and you'll receive a Tikkie request to complete the payment. It's a safe and hassle-free way to shop online.

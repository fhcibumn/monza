DIY for Beginners in Animal Crossing New Horizons

Animal Crossing: New Horizons has been a popular game since its release in 2020. One of the most exciting features of the game is the ability to craft your items through the DIY recipe system. However, for beginners, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we’ll cover some essential DIYs for beginners to get started with.

Fishing Rod

Fishing is a significant part of Animal Crossing, and having a Fishing Rod is essential to catch fish. To craft a Fishing Rod, you’ll need five tree branches and one iron nugget. Once you have these items, head to a workbench and select the Fishing Rod recipe. This DIY is perfect for beginners as it is easy to obtain the required items and can be used to catch fish, which can sell for a good price.

Net

The net is another essential tool for beginners to have. It is used to catch bugs, which can also be sold for a good price. To craft a Net, you’ll need five tree branches and two iron nuggets. Once you have these items, head to a workbench and select the Net recipe.

Simple DIY Workbench

A DIY workbench is essential for crafting items. To craft a Simple DIY Workbench, you’ll need five wood and one iron nugget. Once you have these items, head to a workbench and select the Simple DIY Workbench recipe. This DIY is perfect for beginners as it is easy to obtain the required items and can be used to craft other essential items.

Axe

The Axe is an essential tool for chopping down trees and gathering wood. To craft an Axe, you’ll need five tree branches and one iron nugget. Once you have these items, head to a workbench and select the Axe recipe.

Watering Can

The Watering Can is essential for watering flowers and growing plants. To craft a Watering Can, you’ll need five tree branches and one iron nugget. Once you have these items, head to a workbench and select the Watering Can recipe. This DIY is perfect for beginners as it is easy to obtain the required items and can be used to grow flowers, which can be used in other DIY recipes.

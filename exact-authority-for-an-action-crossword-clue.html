Exact Authority for an Action Crossword Clue

In the world of crossword puzzles, there are few things more frustrating than coming across a clue that you just can't seem to solve. One such clue that has been stumping puzzle enthusiasts for years is "exact authority for an action". This seemingly simple clue has left many people scratching their heads, wondering what the answer could possibly be.

Well, the answer to this clue is actually quite simple once you know it. The answer is "mandate". A mandate is an official order or commission to do something, and it is often given by a person or group with the authority to do so. In other words, a mandate is an exact authority for an action, which makes it the perfect answer to this crossword clue.

Food from heaven crossword clue

If you're stuck on a crossword puzzle clue that asks for "food from heaven", the answer is likely "manna". Manna is a type of edible substance that was said to have been miraculously provided to the Israelites during their journey through the desert in the Bible.

Popular search engine crossword clue

If you're trying to solve a crossword puzzle clue that asks for the capital of Italy, the answer is "Rome". Rome is one of the oldest cities in the world and is known for its rich history, culture, and architecture, including famous landmarks like the Colosseum and the Vatican City.

Small songbirds crossword clue

If you're stuck on a crossword puzzle clue that asks for small songbirds, the answer is likely "finches". Finches are a group of small, seed-eating birds that are known for their beautiful songs and colorful plumage. They are often kept as pets and are popular among birdwatchers around the world.

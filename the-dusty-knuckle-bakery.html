The Dusty Knuckle Bakery: A Hidden Gem in 2023

As we step into the year 2023, London's culinary scene continues to thrive with new hidden gems popping up all over the city. One such gem is The Dusty Knuckle Bakery, a quaint little bakery tucked away in Hackney.

The bakery was founded by Max Tobias and Rebecca Oliver in 2014, and since then, it has become a popular spot for locals and tourists alike. The Dusty Knuckle Bakery is known for its delicious bread, made using traditional baking techniques and high-quality ingredients. They also offer a variety of pastries, cakes, and other baked goods.

Bread Making Classes

One of the unique experiences offered by The Dusty Knuckle Bakery is their bread making classes. These classes are perfect for anyone who wants to learn the art of bread making and experience the joy of baking their own bread. The classes are taught by experienced bakers and cover everything from mixing the dough to baking the final product.

The classes are held in the bakery itself, giving participants a behind-the-scenes look at how the bakery operates. The classes are also interactive, allowing participants to ask questions and get hands-on experience. At the end of the class, participants get to take home their own freshly baked bread.

Community Outreach

Aside from baking delicious bread and offering classes, The Dusty Knuckle Bakery is also committed to giving back to the community. They regularly partner with local charities and organizations to donate their baked goods and raise funds for important causes.

The bakery also offers apprenticeships to young people from disadvantaged backgrounds, giving them the opportunity to learn valuable skills and gain work experience. This commitment to the community is part of what makes The Dusty Knuckle Bakery such a special place.

Event Catering

Looking for a unique catering option for your next event? Look no further than The Dusty Knuckle Bakery. They offer a range of catering options, from breakfast pastries to artisanal sandwiches to indulgent cakes and desserts.

Their catering options are all made using the same high-quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques as their everyday baked goods. Whether you're hosting a small gathering or a large event, The Dusty Knuckle Bakery has something delicious to offer.

Local Ingredients

At The Dusty Knuckle Bakery, they believe in using the freshest, highest-quality ingredients possible. That's why they source their ingredients locally whenever possible. From flour to fruit to dairy, they work with local farmers and producers to ensure that their ingredients are of the highest quality and support the local community.

This commitment to local ingredients not only ensures the quality of their baked goods but also helps support the local economy and reduce their carbon footprint.

Online Ordering

Can't make it to the bakery in person? No problem. The Dusty Knuckle Bakery offers online ordering, so you can enjoy their delicious baked goods no matter where you are.

From their famous sourdough bread to their decadent pastries and cakes, everything is available for online ordering. Simply place your order on their website and have it delivered straight to your doorstep.

Bakery Merchandise

If you're a fan of The Dusty Knuckle Bakery, you can show your support by purchasing their merchandise. From t-shirts to tote bags to enamel pins, there's something for everyone.

Not only do their merchandise options allow you to show off your love for the bakery, but they also make great gifts for the foodies in your life.

Machete Cocina Mexicana Menu 2023

If you're a fan of Mexican cuisine, you won't want to miss out on the Machete Cocina Mexicana menu. This popular restaurant chain has been serving up delicious dishes for years, and their 2023 menu is sure to impress. From classic favorites to innovative new creations, there's something for everyone on the Machete Cocina Mexicana menu.

So what can you expect to find on the Machete Cocina Mexicana menu this year? Let's take a closer look.

Appetizers

Start your meal off right with one of Machete Cocina Mexicana's delicious appetizers. The 2023 menu features classic options like nachos and guacamole, as well as more unique dishes like chicharrones and elote callejero. Don't miss out on the queso fundido, a cheesy dip served with a side of tortillas for dipping.

Entrees

When it comes to entrees, Machete Cocina Mexicana has something for everyone. Meat lovers will enjoy the carne asada or the pollo en mole, while vegetarians can indulge in the vegetariano burrito or the enchiladas de espinacas. Seafood lovers won't want to miss the camarones al ajillo, a dish featuring shrimp in a garlic sauce.

Desserts

No meal is complete without a sweet treat, and Machete Cocina Mexicana has plenty of options to choose from. The tres leches cake is a classic favorite, while the churros con cajeta offer a unique twist on a traditional dessert. And for those looking for something a bit lighter, the fruit plate is a refreshing option.

Drinks

To wash it all down, Machete Cocina Mexicana offers a variety of drinks to choose from. Their margaritas are a customer favorite, but if you're not in the mood for alcohol, the horchata or agua fresca are both great options. And for those who want to try something new, the tamarindo margarita is a must-try.

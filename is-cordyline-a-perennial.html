Cordyline is a plant that belongs to the Asparagaceae family. It is also known as Ti plant, and it is native to Southeast Asia and Polynesia. Perennial or Annual?

When it comes to cordyline, the answer is both. Cordyline is a perennial plant, which means that it can live for more than two years. In warmer climates, cordyline can be grown as a perennial, and it will continue to thrive year after year.

Whether you are growing cordyline as an annual or a perennial, it is important to provide it with the right care. Cordyline prefers well-draining soil and partial shade.

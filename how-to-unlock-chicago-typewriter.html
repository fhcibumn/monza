How to Unlock Chicago Typewriter

Are you a fan of the Resident Evil franchise? If so, you might have heard of the legendary Chicago Typewriter, a powerful submachine gun that can tear through hordes of zombies with ease. However, unlocking this weapon is no easy feat. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps needed to obtain the Chicago Typewriter in Resident Evil 4.

Step 1: Beat the Game

The first step to unlocking the Chicago Typewriter is to beat the main story mode of Resident Evil 4. This might seem like a daunting task, but don't worry - there are plenty of resources available online to help you get through the game. Once you've beaten the final boss, you'll unlock a new game mode called "Separate Ways."

Step 2: Beat Separate Ways

In "Separate Ways," you play as Ada Wong, a mysterious spy who's on a mission to retrieve a sample of the Las Plagas parasite. This mode offers a different perspective on the events of Resident Evil 4, and it also contains a new boss fight and some unique cutscenes. Once you've beaten "Separate Ways," you'll unlock the Chicago Typewriter for purchase in the main game.

Step 3: Save Up Your Money

The Chicago Typewriter is an expensive weapon, costing a whopping 1 million pesetas (the in-game currency). You'll need to save up your money by selling treasures and unused items, and by completing side missions and challenges. It might take some time, but it's worth it - the Chicago Typewriter is one of the most powerful weapons in the game.

Step 4: Enjoy Your New Weapon

Once you've purchased the Chicago Typewriter, you'll be able to mow down enemies with ease. This weapon has a high rate of fire and deals massive damage, making it perfect for taking on tough enemies and bosses. Just be careful not to waste your ammo - the Chicago Typewriter has a limited supply, and you won't be able to purchase more once you've run out.

Conclusion

Unlocking the Chicago Typewriter in Resident Evil 4 is a challenging but rewarding task. By following these steps and putting in some effort, you'll be able to obtain one of the most powerful weapons in the game. Happy hunting!
